Based on your description, this appears to be **lichen planus**—a chronic inflammatory skin condition. It typically presents with intensely itchy, purple-red, flat-topped bumps that can evolve into discolored or depressed scars over time, matching your details closely. A dermatologist can confirm this with a visual exam or biopsy and recommend treatments like topical steroids or light therapy. Avoid scratching, as it worsens scarring.
